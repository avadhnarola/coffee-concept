How to Choose the Perfect Coffee Beans

Choosing the right coffee beans can transform your daily cup into a rich, flavorful experience. From aroma to taste, every detail matters when selecting beans that suit your brewing style.

Roast Level: Light roasts preserve natural flavors and acidity, medium roasts provide balance, and dark roasts create bold, smoky richness. Choose based on your taste preference.

Freshness: Always check the roast date. Freshly roasted beans (within 2–4 weeks) provide the best aroma and flavor. Store them in an airtight container away from heat and light.
